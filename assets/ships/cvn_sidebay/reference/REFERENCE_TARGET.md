# CVN Sidebay Reference Target

The adjacent `carrier_reference_sheet.png` is the visual authority for the
authored Sidebay carrier. The production model keeps existing gameplay
contracts while translating them into the reference's design language.

## Required silhouette

- 76 m wide, 32 m high, and 220 m long, matching the runtime carrier catalog.
- Long, low, fully enclosed armored slab with a rounded/faceted wedge bow.
- No exposed dorsal runway. The top surface is continuous armor.
- Broad armored stern with six functional drives arranged 3 × 2. The reference
  shows eight, but six is the established Sidebay gameplay contract.
- Low, terraced command citadel aft of center with bridge windows, antennae,
  and sensor masts.

## Required carrier features

- Two visually dominant open hangar galleries on each side, separated by an
  armored midship pier. Three launch sockets per side remain available inside
  those two larger galleries.
- Heavy segmented blast doors that visibly stow for flight operations and seal
  after launch/recovery traffic clears.
- Ten linked PDW/flak cannon instances distributed sparsely along the dorsal
  armor edges.
- Dark interior hangar surfaces, blue approach/service lights, internal deck
  rails, and parked fighter silhouettes.

## Surface language

- Dense overlapping armor plates at several scales rather than large smooth
  placeholder surfaces.
- Reinforced edge bands, recessed service trenches, vents, access covers,
  sparse blue pin lights, and restrained metallic variation.
- Dark naval gunmetal with rough, armored surfaces; emissive elements are
  limited to engines, bridge windows, hangars, and navigation details.

## Acceptance evidence

- Blender source and exported GLB match the dimensions and socket contract.
- Multi-angle renders cover top, side, bow, stern, and three-quarter views.
- Open and closed hangar-door states are visibly distinct.
- Godot asset validation and carrier launch/recovery integration tests pass.

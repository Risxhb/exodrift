Command doctrine

You are not above the battle.
You are inside it.

Take the helm of a heavy carrier while issuing orders to persistent wings and capital escorts. Fight command strikes, interceptions, defenses, escorts, captures, and extractions in one uninterrupted battlespace.

01

Fly the flagship

Command a textured 220-meter carrier with heavy persistent throttle, double-click vector flight, six retractable fighter galleries, a dorsal drone hive, lock-directed flak walls, guided salvos, one nuclear torpedo, automated defenses, and layered durability.

02

Direct the fleet

Open a live holographic map without pausing. Use fixed fleet groups, a target-sensitive command wheel, queued paths, formations, doctrine, spacing, and link-aware orders while the carrier holds course.

03

Own the flight cycle

Launch six named four-craft Raptor squadrons independently and task a three-drone Watcher EW wing. Track each group’s health, strength, endurance, recovery, service, and physical redeployment.

Ordnance directive

Lock the sector.
Raise the wall.

Three deliberate weapon directors turn the Sidebay into a carrier-scale weapons platform. Each system has a clear job, a readable footprint, and a cost to committing it.

1

FLAK FIRING SOLUTION

Raise the wall of flak

Lock a contact and saturate its bearing with seven explosive airbursts. Missiles and strikecraft are shredded; capital ships take light damage, and friendlies must clear the sector.

2

STRIKE DIRECTOR

Commit guided salvos

Lock a resolved contact and release a visible multi-missile strike with persistent trails.

3

STRATEGIC DIRECTOR

One nuclear torpedo

Arm one run-defining weapon, respect its blast radius, and spend it only when the formation justifies the risk.
